# Calculation Model

## Core formulas

### Effort with automation

```text
Automated Effort = Baseline Effort × (1 - Efficiency Gain)
```

### Daily cost

```text
Daily Cost = Items per Day × Effort per Item × Labor Rate
```

### Annual savings

```text
Annual Savings = (Current Daily Cost × Work Days) - (Automated Daily Cost × Work Days)
```

### Realized benefits

```text
Realized Benefits = Annual Savings × Number of Years
```

### Total investment

Total investment is computed from:

- Year 1 software cost
- Year 1 service cost
- Recurring support cost with annual escalation

### Net savings

```text
Net Savings = Realized Benefits - Total Investment
```

### ROI

```text
ROI % = (Net Savings / Total Investment) × 100
```

### Payback period

```text
Payback Period (months) = (Year 1 Investment / Annual Savings) × 12
```

## Notes

The calculator uses live browser-side JavaScript to update all visuals and tables whenever an input changes.
